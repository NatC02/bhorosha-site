Bhorosha

A job sourcing program for the informal sector

About.

Bhorosha is a Non-Profit organization following the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) to empower woman by giving them job opportunities.
Our team is personally contributing to Sustainable Development GOAL 1: No Poverty, GOAL 8: Decent Work and Economic Growth, and GOAL 9: Industry, Innovation and Infrastructure.
To learn more about SDGs visit: The United Nations Sustainable Development Goals.
If you would like to help in any way possible, please consider donating to our Nonprofit Organization Website .

Bangladesh is a South Asian country to the east of India where the majority of women workers in Bangladesh are employed in the informal sector or struggling to look for jobs. Bhorosha is an initiative to empower women in third world countries by giving them a source of income by connecting potential employees to employers based on their skills to jobs from relevant industries or to provide unskilled women with necessary training to land a job. We are constantly keeping an eye out for those who are willing to support us. By creating a job listing page, we will be able to give those who are looking for jobs a chance and opportunity to make a living.

What we do.

Provide skills-gap training
Increase prospective employee pool through micro-agents
We provide a cloud-based data solution
